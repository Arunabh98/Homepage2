XLR8!!

In this competition we (including 2 other team members and me) had to design a four-wheel electronic car which could be controlled by a remote control (which was a mobile phone app). Our robot had to clear a track which had different kinds of obstacles.

CONCEPTS LEARNT

  1. How to control motors using L293D, the motor driver
  2. Using IC 7805 (to provide a constant, regulated source of power)
  3. How to use bluetooth modules to control the robot through our phones
